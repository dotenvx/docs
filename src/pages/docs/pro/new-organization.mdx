# New organization
<GuideHeading lead={description} />

## CLI üìü

In your cli, run the **settings orgnew** command.

```bash
$ dotenvx pro settings orgnew
```

Follow the prompts to **create your organization**. For example, I am creating my [motdotla](https://github.com/motdotla) account in the screenshot below.

<ScreenshotBrowser url='https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/52909ed6-6ee5-4b3a-9e2e-b535674326ac' www='pro.dotenvx.com' width='md:w-4/6' />

<Note>
  It must be an organization (or account) **you control** on GitHub.
</Note>

On the next screen, it will show **created successfully** with your organization listed.

<ScreenshotBrowser url='https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/45105b51-2c56-4e1b-96a2-5c85ad8c84ed' www='pro.dotenvx.com' width='md:w-4/6' />

Click through to **view your organization**.

<ScreenshotBrowser url='https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e20d97dc-c6fb-4d73-85aa-f78b9098e014' www='pro.dotenvx.com' width='md:w-4/6' />

Nice nice. You're ready to add a project next.
