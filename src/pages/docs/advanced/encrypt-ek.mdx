##### [Advanced](/docs/advanced)
# dotenvx encrypt -ek

<p className="lead">{description}</p>

```sh
$ echo "HELLO=World\nHELLO2=Universe" > .env

$ dotenvx encrypt -ek HELLO
✔ encrypted (.env)
```

Even specify a glob pattern.

```sh
$ echo "HELLO=World\nHOLA=Mundo" > .env

$ dotenvx encrypt -ek "HO*"
✔ encrypted (.env)
```
